How does this work

Everything you need to know before you get started

You submit the details of your requirement, project, technology stack, etc. through our Contact Us form.

Based on the request and details provided, an expert will be assigned to go over the requirements in detail. This happens through a voice call or chat in whatever way you are comfortable.

The expert will then assess the task. Only if the expert is 100% confident that he will be able to help and contribute fully to the project, he will accept the assignment.

Once you and the technical expert agree to proceed further, provided you are comfortable working with him, you can transfer the money to the expert's account.

You and the assigned expert can start communicating on the project related needs once the transfer is complete. That's all. No 3rd party involvement or complicated processes.
